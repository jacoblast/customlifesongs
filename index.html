About Custom Life Songs

I am passionate about capturing the essence of an individual through music! I love talking to people about their lives and loved ones. Through these conversations, I learn what makes each person unique and special, and synthesize what people say into personalized songs.

I allow the people to choose the mood of music that resonates with them, whether it's joyful, reflective, nostalgic, etc. My mission is to create a lasting musical tribute that honors a life journey, preserving their memories in a beautiful and meaningful way.

Whether you are a family wanting to commemorate your parent's life, or someone wanting to leave a legacy song for your children, I'd love to help. Let's create a soundtrack to your story together!
