Legacy Projects

A legacy project is orchestrated by the individual the song is for, and/or any loved ones who would like to be involved. This can look a variety of ways, including live interviews, and/or having loved ones fill out questionnaires, depending on the number of people involved. You may involve as many people's input as you wish.

The interviews and questionnaires are targeted at finding out the most important details of the person's life. I will synthesize all of the answers, pulling out the most important themes and memories in order to create a fully unique song, tailored to tell the story of a life. These are great group gifts! I am happy to come and perform your song, and you can even choose to have it professionally recorded and produced!
