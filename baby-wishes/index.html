Baby Wishes

Every parent has wishes for their child, and I will help you put these into song. I will interview you about the types of things you would like to say to your child, and will synthesize what you say into melody and lyrics for them to hear as they grow older, and for you all to share together as a family. We can include as many people from your family and friends as you like in this process.
